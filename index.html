About Shenehaa

I'm Shenehaa Sree, a freelance Graphic Designer and Bachelor of Graphic Design student at City University Malaysia. Passionate about visual storytelling and creative communication, I specialize in creating impactful designs that combine aesthetics with purpose.

Since 2023, I have worked with individual and business clients to design professional illustrations, logos, posters, pamphlets, ceremony cards, and digital visual assets using industry-standard tools such as Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, and Canva.

My design approach focuses on branding, layout composition, colour theory, and visual communication while incorporating strong artistic foundations in drawing and painting. I enjoy transforming ideas into visually engaging designs that leave a lasting impression and effectively communicate a brand's identity.

I am continuously exploring new creative trends and techniques to improve my craft and deliver modern, meaningful, and professional design solutions.
